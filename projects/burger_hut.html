Project Overview

Burger Hut is a sleek and modern restaurant website designed to create a strong online presence for a burger joint. With a visually appealing layout, it immediately captures visitors' attention with high-quality imagery and bold typography. The hero section highlights a delicious burger, enticing customers to explore more.

The website features a clean and intuitive navigation menu, allowing users to browse the menu, check out specials, and place orders effortlessly. A prominent call-to-action button encourages quick online ordering, enhancing customer convenience.

Designed with a fully responsive layout, the website ensures a seamless experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Key sections include an "About Us" page, a well-structured menu display, and a contact section for easy customer interaction.

This project showcases my ability to develop visually engaging, user-friendly, and functional websites. It reflects my attention to detail and commitment to creating designs that enhance user experience and business growth.
